This page explains how to request deletion of your personal data collected through [Your App Name].

How to Request Data Deletion

If you have used our app via Facebook and wish to delete your data from our servers, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook account’s Settings & Privacy > Apps and Websites.
  2. Find and select [Your App Name].
  3. Click “Remove” to disconnect the app from your account.
  4. To formally request data deletion, send an email to: support@yourdomain.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

In your email, please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Facebook account
  • Any other information that may help us locate your data

Once we receive your request, we will process the deletion within 30 days and confirm completion via email.
